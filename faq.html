What is Surehealth card?

This is a prepaid health card for any individual that offers unlimited consultation for 1 year.

Is there an age limit for Surehealth card?

For Adult 18 – 100 years old.

Is there an age limit for Kiddie Kit card?

For Kids 0 – 17 years old.

Are you accredited with any Hospitals?

We are not accredited with any hospitals as we are a one stop shop clinic that can cater to your outpatient needs.

Is this like an HMO Card?

No, unlike an HMO we do not have inpatient and hospital services. This is a prepaid health card exclusive to any Aventus Medical Care, Inc. branches.

Can this card cover my whole family?

Yes, you can purchase a card for each family member. The variants may vary depending on what each member may need.

How do I apply for the Surehealth card?

Fill up application form which you can get in any Aventus Clinic branches or online, Submit / Upload a 1 x 1 picture and photocopy of any valid ID.

What are the different packages?

You can choose from any of our 6 different packages. For complete details, you can visit our website at www.aventusmedical.com.

What is your MOP (Mode of Payment)?

You can pay in cash at any Aventus Clinic branches or deposit the payment at the listed banks. Once paid the card is activated for 1 year.

Is the 20% discount in Surehealth card (prime) on top of the senior citizen?

No, only one discount will apply for any given single transactions.

Do you cover emergency care/hospital availments?

No, as we are not accredited with any trauma centers or hospitals.

Who can purchase / be enrolled under the Surehealth Cards?

Any individual who wishes to have outpatient medical coverage.
